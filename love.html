Our Love Story

On November 11, 2023, our journey together began. At first, we reconnected as friends, but it didn't take long for our bond to blossom into something special. Within a few months, we became inseparable best friends. Then, as the days turned into weeks and weeks into months, I found myself falling deeply in love with her. With butterflies in my stomach and all the courage I could muster, I confessed my feelings on February 10, 2024. To my immense joy, she said yes, and our love story began to unfold from that moment on.
